PMLA Section 12:
Financial institutions must maintain records of transactions and report suspicious activity to authorities.

Structuring:
Breaking large transactions into smaller amounts to evade reporting thresholds is considered suspicious behaviour.

RBI KYC Guidelines:
Banks must monitor transactions for unusual patterns including rapid transfers and repeated near-threshold deposits.

FIU-IND Suspicious Transaction Reporting:
Banks must file STR reports for transactions suspected to involve money laundering or financial crime.
